Beautiful World

Photography

Photography is what the author wants to capture. The beauty that lurks in his eyes. In this section I want to introduce you to my works. Everyone has a dream: someone needs a car, someone needs a cool smartphone, and I've always dreamed of a camera. And I bought it, in installments, but I bought it. And now, at any convenient opportunity, I take him for a walk with me. Wandering the streets of cities or relaxing in nature, we often miss small moments that reveal our world in a different way. They remind us that there are a lot of beautiful things in the world, both in small things and in ordinary things. Sometimes you can scare off a beautiful frame and get something like that. It's all about the skull of the photographer. Since I have little skill, I photograph only for myself, but I am not ashamed of my works and show them.

Hunting for the beautiful

I find inspiration in the photos to create and fight on. This is my way of relaxation, to leave depressing thoughts or currently unfulfilled plans. As a hunter, I track down a beautiful plan and transfer my thoughts to it.
